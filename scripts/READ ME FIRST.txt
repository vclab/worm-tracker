ParaTracker: How to open on macOS
====================================

1) Drag ParaTracker into the Applications folder (shown in this window).

2) Open your Applications folder in Finder.

3) RIGHT-CLICK on ParaTracker and choose "Open".
   (Do not double-click the first time; macOS will block it.)

4) In the dialog that appears, click "Open".

You only need to do this once. After that, ParaTracker opens like any
other app when you double-click it.


Why is this step needed?
------------------------

macOS blocks apps whose developers have not paid Apple's yearly $99
Developer Program fee. ParaTracker is free research software, so we
have not paid that fee. The right-click and Open step tells macOS
that you trust the app, and it never asks again.
